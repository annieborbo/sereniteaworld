Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tartary Buckwheat Tea?
Tartary Buckwheat Tea, also known as Soba tea, is made from roasted buckwheat groats. Despite the name, it's not related to wheat and is naturally gluten-free. It has a pleasant, nutty flavor and has been enjoyed for centuries in Asia for its health benefits.
Is it really caffeine-free?
Yes! Our Tartary Buckwheat Tea is 100% naturally caffeine-free, making it perfect for enjoying any time of day, including before bedtime.
How do I brew buckwheat tea?
Use 1-2 teaspoons of loose tea or one tea bag per cup. Pour hot water (90-100°C) over the tea and steep for 3-5 minutes. The tea can be re-steeped 2-3 times.
Is it safe for people with gluten intolerance?
Yes! Despite having "wheat" in its name, buckwheat is not related to wheat and is naturally gluten-free. Our products are also processed in a gluten-free facility.
